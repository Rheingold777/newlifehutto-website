About Us

A Spirit-filled Apostolic community of believers

Who We Are

At New Life Hutto, we are more than just a gathering. We are an Apostolic, Spirit-filled community of believers united in faith, growing together in the power of the Holy Ghost.

The Bible says, "Iron sharpeneth iron" (Proverbs 27:17), and we believe that through fellowship, prayer, and the Word, we strengthen one another and draw closer to God.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic, Spirit-filled church where people experience the one true God, receive the gift of the Holy Ghost, are baptized in Jesus' name, and are empowered to live transformed lives.

Our Mission

Reach souls with Apostolic truth.
Connect people to God through Spirit-filled worship.
Renew lives through the transforming power of Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

New Life Hutto is affiliated with the United Pentecostal Church International. We are committed to teaching God's Word to the entire family and believe that strong families create a strong church.

Our purpose is to preach and teach the Word of Faith, causing faith to rise in the heart and allowing the believer to walk in success in every area of their life.
